About Us

Hello! Welcome to Paradiso. Our name was inspired by the movie "Cinema Paradiso." This project began as an initiative to end the need to choose a movie when there are so many different options out there. You can get a randomly generated movie showing the title, release date, rating, and summary. You can also narrow your results by genre.

We are The Pink Ladies, named after the type of apple and not the Grease girl group. Our team is composed of three people: Mabel Felix, Corine Lu, and Adrian Mendoza. We are currently students as part of Google's Computer Science Summer Academy and this is our final project.

The information available comes from The Movie DB.
